# Licensing Overview

DATA2DNA uses a dual licensing model.

## Option 1: AGPL-3.0 (Free)

Use DATA2DNA freely if:

- ✓ Your project is open source
- ✓ You publish all modifications
- ✓ Network use of the software triggers open-source obligations

Full text: [LICENSE](LICENSE)

## Option 2: Commercial License (Paid)

Required if:

- ✗ You cannot open-source your product
- ✗ You are building a proprietary SaaS on top of DATA2DNA
- ✗ Your company's legal policy prohibits AGPL

Contact vedcimit@gmail.com for commercial terms.  
Details: [LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.md](LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.md)

## Why This Model?

DNA storage infrastructure should be open science. But building and maintaining
production-grade open source requires resources. The dual license ensures both
goals are served:

1. **Researchers and open-source developers** get full access at no cost
2. **Companies building proprietary products** fund continued development

This is the same model used by MongoDB, Qt, Grafana, and others.
